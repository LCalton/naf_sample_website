Meet the Companions!

Though every dog is different, we generally group our dogs into "Athletes" and "Companions." All border collies need exercise, and all border collies can make excellent pets, but Athletes really require heavy doses of activity, and Companions require a little less--usually because they're older dogs.

What does it cost?

All potential new "dog parents" must complete an application and may be asked some pointed questions. This is because border collies require the right home. We're proud that we've never had to take a dog back!

A re-homing fee of $150 will be charged for a successful adoption. This doesn't make much of a dent in the cost of rescuing a dog, but it does help ensure only folks serious about finding "their" dog apply.
